About us

Come and enjoy the ultimate dinning Experience like no other at Goa's Favourite Restaurant!

Silverleaf-GOA – an award winning fine dinning restaurant with bar – is located on Chogm Road. Our mission at Silverleaf-GOA is to celebrate the bounty of flavours as family and friends gather to share a good time over a great meal. We promise to entertain you with a feast not only for your eyes, but your taste buds as well.
